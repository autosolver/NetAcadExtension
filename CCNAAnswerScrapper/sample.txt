                                        <h3>Which characteristic describes an IPv6 enhancement over IPv4?</h3>
                                        <ul>
                                            <li>IPv6 addresses are based on 128-bit flat addressing as opposed to IPv4
                                                which is based on 32-bit hierarchical addressing.</li>
                                            <li><span style="color: #ff0000;">The IPv6 header is simpler than the IPv4
                                                    header is, which improves packet handling.</span></li>
                                            <li>Both IPv4 and IPv6 support authentication, but only IPv6 supports
                                                privacy capabilities.</li>
                                            <li>The IPv6 address space is four times bigger than the IPv4 address
                                                space.<br />
